Ahri: The Nine-Tailed Fox Middle

Essence Theft

Whenever Ahri hits an enemy with one of her abilities, she gains a charge of Essence Theft, and can gain up to 3 charges per ability cast. Upon reaching 9 charges, her next ability heals her for 3/5/9/18 (+9% Ability Power) for each enemy hit.

Orb of Deception
Range: 880 Cooldown: 7
Cost: 65/70/75/80 Mana

Ahri sends out and pulls back her orb, dealing magic damage on the way out and true damage on the way back. Ahri gains movement speed that decays while her orb is traveling.

Deals 40/65/90/115/140 (+35% Ability Power) magic damage on the way out, and 40/65/90/115/140 (+35% Ability Power) true damage on the way back. Ahri gains movement speed that decays while her orb is traveling.

Fox-Fire
Range: 700 Cooldown: 9/8/7/6/5
Cost: 50 Mana

Releases three fox-fires that lock on to nearby enemies (prioritizes Champions) dealing 40/65/90/115/140 (+40% Ability Power) magic damage.Enemies hit with multiple fox-fires take 30% damage from each additional fox-fire beyond the first, for a maximum of 0 damage to a single enemy.

Charm
Range: 975 Cooldown: 12
Cost: 85 Mana

Ahri blows a kiss dealing 60/95/130/165/200 (+50% Ability Power) magic damage and charms an enemy causing them to walk harmlessly towards Ahri for 1/1.25/1.5/1.75/2 second(s).

Spirit Rush
Range: 450 Cooldown: 110/95/80
Cost: 100 Mana

Ahri nimbly dashes forward firing 3 essence bolts at nearby enemies (prioritizes Champions) dealing 70/110/150 (+30% Ability Power) magic damage. Can be cast up to three times within 10 seconds before going on cooldown.
