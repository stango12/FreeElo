Urgot: The Headsman's Pride Top/Marksman

Zaun-Touched Bolt Augmenter

Urgot's basic attacks and Acid Hunters reduce his targets' damage by 15% for 2.5 seconds.

Acid Hunter
Range: 1000 Cooldown: 2
Cost: 40 Mana

Urgot fires a missile toward the cursor that deals 10/40/70/100/130 (+85% Attack Damage) physical damage. If Acid Hunter kills a unit half its mana cost is refunded. Missile-lock can be achieved by holding the cursor over a target afflicted by Noxian Corrosive Charge.

Terror Capacitor
Cooldown: 16/15/14/13/12 Cost: 55/60/65/70/75 Mana

Urgot charges up his terror capacitor to gain a shield that absorbs 60/100/140/180/220 (+80% Ability Power) (+8% of his maximum mana) damage for 5 seconds. While the shield is active, Urgot's attacks and missiles slow targets by 20/25/30/35/40%.

Noxian Corrosive Charge
Range: 900 Cooldown: 15/14/13/12/11
Cost: 50/55/60/65/70 Mana

Urgot launches a Corrosive Charge at a target location. Enemies afflicted by the charge have their Armor reduced by 12/14/16/18/20% and take 75/130/185/240/295 (+60% bonus Attack Damage) physical damage over 5 seconds. Acid Hunter is able to missile-lock onto targets affected by Noxian Corrosive Charge.

Hyper-Kinetic Position Reverser
Range: 550/700/850 Cooldown: 120/100/80
Cost: 100 Mana

Urgot targets an enemy champion and channels his Hyper-Kinetic Position Reverser for 1 second, swapping locations with his target afterward and terrifying nearby enemies for 1.5 seconds. His target is suppressed for the duration of the channel. Urgot takes 30/40/50% reduced damage for 5 seconds beginning at the start of the channel, and his target is slowed by 40% for 3 seconds after being swapped.
