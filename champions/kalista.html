Kalista: The Spear of Vengeance Marksman

Martial Poise

Enter a movement command while winding up Kalista's basic attack or Pierce to lunge a short distance when she launches her attack.

Pierce
Range: 1150 Cooldown: 8
Cost: 50/55/60/65/70 Mana

Kalista hurls a fast but narrow spear that deals 10/70/130/190/250 (+100% Attack Damage) physical damage. Triggers Martial Poise (P), Sentinel (W), and Rend (E). If it kills a target, Pierce continues onward, passing all stacks of Rend to the next target.

Sentinel
Range: 5000 Cooldown: 30
Recharge Time: 90/80/70/60/50 Cost: 20 Mana

Passive: When Kalista is near her Oathsworn, she gains Attack Speed. If they both basic attack the same target, she deals 5/7.5/10/12.5/15% of their max health as magic damage. There is a 10 second cooldown per target.

Active: Send a soul Sentinel to patrol an unseen area. Champions spotted are revealed for 4 seconds. Sentinels last 3 laps. Kalista gains a charge of Sentinel every 90/80/70/60/50 seconds.

Rend
Range: 1000 Cooldown: 14/12.5/11/9.5/8
Cost: 30 Mana

Passive: While Rend is off cooldown, Kalista's spears pierce their target, lingering for 4 seconds.

Active: Rip the spears from nearby targets, dealing 20/30/40/50/60 (+60% Attack Damage) physical damage and slowing their Movement Speed by 25/30/35/40/45% for 2 seconds. If Rend kills at least one target, its cooldown is reset. If Rend kills two or more targets, its mana cost is refunded.

Fate's Call
Range: 1000 Cooldown: 120/90/60
Cost: 100 Mana

Kalista draws her Oathsworn to her. For up to 4 seconds, the Oathsworn is untargetable and pacified. The Oathsworn may mouse click to fly toward a target position, stopping at the first enemy champion hit and knocking all enemies in a small radius back. Kalista's Oathsworn must be within 1100 units for her to cast this ability.
