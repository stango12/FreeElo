Dr. Mundo: The Madman of Zaun Top/Jungle

Adrenaline Rush

Dr. Mundo regenerates 0.3% of his maximum Health each second.

Infected Cleaver
Range: 975 Cooldown: 4
Cost: 50/60/70/80/90 Health

Dr. Mundo hurls his cleaver, dealing magic damage equal to 15/17.5/20/22.5/25% of the target's current Health and slowing them by 40% for 2 seconds. Infected Cleaver does 80/130/180/230/280 minimum, 300/350/400/450/500 maximum damage to Monsters. Half of the Health cost is refunded if the cleaver hits an enemy. The full Health cost is refunded if the cleaver kills an enemy.

Burning Agony
Range: 325 Cooldown: 4
Cost: 10/15/20/25/30 Health Per Second

Toggle: Dr. Mundo deals 35/50/65/80/95 (+20% Ability Power) magic damage to nearby enemies per second, and reduces the duration of disables on Dr. Mundo by 10/15/20/25/30%.

Masochism
Range: 150 Cooldown: 6
Cost: 25/35/45/55/65 Health

Dr. Mundo deals an additional 3/3.5/4/4.5/5% of maximum health as physical damage on his next basic attack and gains 30/50/70/90/110 Attack Damage for 5 seconds. Dr. Mundo gains an additional +0.3/0.5/0.7/0.9/1.1 Attack Damage for each percent of his Health he is missing.

Sadism
Cooldown: 100/85/70 Cost: 20% Current Health

Dr. Mundo regenerates 40/50/60% of maximum health over 12 seconds. Additionally, he gains 15/25/35% movement speed during this time.
