Zyra: Rise of the Thorns Support

Garden of Thorns

Seeds spawn around Zyra periodically, becoming faster with level and lasting 45 seconds. If an enemy Champion steps on a seed, it dies.

Zyra can cast spells near seeds to grow plants. Extra plants striking the same target deal reduced damage.

Deadly Spines
Range: 800 Cooldown: 7/6.5/6/5.5/5
Cost: 70 Mana

Zyra sprouts thick vines that spread through the ground and explode into spines, dealing 60/95/130/165/200 (+60% Ability Power) magic damage to enemies within the area.

Garden of Thorns: If Deadly Spines is cast near a seed, a Thorn Spitter grows, dealing 29-114 (+15% Ability Power) magic damage. A Thorn Spitter has 750 range and lasts 5-7.5 seconds.

Rampant Growth
Range: 850 Recharge Time: 20/18/16/14/12
Cost: 1 Seed

Zyra plants a seed, lasting 60 seconds. If an enemy Champion steps on a seed, it dies. Seeds spawned by Rampant Growth grant vision in a small area, and if stepped on they grant vision of the enemy champion for 2 seconds. Zyra stores a seed every 20/18/16/14/12 seconds (Max: 8 seeds planted).

Passive: Zyra's plants gain 10/20/30/40/50% extra maximum Health.

Grasping Roots
Range: 1100 Cooldown: 12
Cost: 70/75/80/85/90 Mana

Zyra sends forward vines dealing 60/95/130/165/200 (+50% Ability Power) magic damage and rooting enemies for 0.75/1/1.25/1.5/1.75 second(s).

Garden of Thorns: If Grasping Roots passes near a seed, a Vine Lasher grows dealing 29-114 (+15% Ability Power) magic damage and slowing enemies by 30% for 2 seconds. A Vine Lasher has 400 range and lasts 5.75 seconds.

Stranglethorns
Range: 700 Cooldown: 130/120/110
Cost: 100/120/140 Mana

Zyra summons the fury of nature, growing a twisted thicket at target location which deals 180/265/350 (+70% Ability Power) magic damage to all enemies in the area as it expands. After 2 seconds, the vines snap upward knocking enemies into the air for 1 second.

Garden of Thorns: Plants within the thicket are enraged, attacking in a flurry for 150% total damage.
