Lulu: The Fae Sorceress Support

Pix, Faerie Companion

Pix is a wild Faerie that accompanies Lulu. Pix will fire a barrage of magical energy at targets that Lulu attacks.

Glitterlance
Range: 925 Cooldown: 7
Cost: 60/65/70/75/80 Mana

Lulu and Pix each fire a piercing bolt dealing 80/125/170/215/260 (+50% Ability Power) magic damage to the first enemy hit and 56/87.5/119/150.0/182 (+35% Ability Power) to all additional enemies. Enemies hit are slowed by 80%, decaying over the next 2 second(s).

Whimsy
Range: 650 Cooldown: 16/15/14/13/12
Cost: 65 Mana

On Ally Cast: Target ally gains 30% (+5% Ability Power) Movement Speed and 25/30/35/40/45% Attack Speed for 3/3.25/3.5/3.75/4 seconds.

On Enemy Cast: Polymorphs an enemy champion for 1.25/1.5/1.75/2/2.25 seconds, disabling their ability to attack or cast spells and reducing their base Movement Speed by 60.

Help, Pix!
Range: 650 Cooldown: 10
Cost: 60/70/80/90/100 Mana

On Ally Cast: Commands Pix to jump to an ally and then follow and aid their attacks instead of Lulu's for 6 seconds. If the ally is a champion, Pix shields them from 80/120/160/200/240 (+60% Ability Power) damage for 6 seconds.

On Enemy Cast: Pix deals 80/110/140/170/200 (+40% Ability Power) magic damage to target enemy unit. Pix then follows and grants vision of them for 4 seconds.

Wild Growth
Range: 90 Cooldown: 110/95/80
Cost: 100 Mana

Lulu enlarges her ally, knocking nearby enemies into the air. For 7 seconds, her ally gains 300/450/600 (+50% Ability Power) bonus health and slows nearby enemies by 30/45/60%.
