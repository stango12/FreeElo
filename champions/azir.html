Azir: The Emperor of the Sands Middle

Shurima's Legacy
Range: 400 Cooldown: 180

INNATE: Azir places a marker above the ruins of all destroyed turrets outside the enemy base.

DISC OF THE SUN: Azir raises the Sun Disc from the selected marked ruins after a brief delay. The Sun Disc functions like a standard turret but gains bonus attack damage equal to 15% AP. It also grants Azir any gold it earns. The Sun Disc loses health over 1 minute and loses its armor if Azir dies or moves too far away.

Conquering Sands
Range: 875 Cooldown: 10/9/8/7/6 Cost: 70 Mana

Azir sends all Sand Soldiers towards a location. Sand Soldiers deal 65/85/105/125/145 (+50%) magic damage to enemies they pass through and apply a 25% slow for 1 second.

Enemies hit by multiple Sand Soldiers will not take additional damage.

Arise!
Range: 450/875 Cooldown: 1.5 Cost: 40 Mana

Passive: Gains 20/30/40/50/60% attack speed.

Active: Azir summons a Sand Soldier for 9 seconds. When Azir attacks an enemy in a soldier's range, the soldier attacks instead of Azir, dealing undefined (+60%) magic damage to enemies in a line. If multiple soldiers strike the same target, each soldier after the first deals 25% damage.

Azir can store up to 2 Sand Soldiers at a time. A new soldier becomes available every 12/11/10/9/8 seconds.

Sand Soldiers can attack targets outside of Azir's basic attack range.
Sand Soldiers deactivate if they are too far away from Azir.
Sand Soldiers expire twice as fast when near an enemy turret.

Shifting Sands
Range: 1100 Cooldown: 19/18/17/16/15 Cost: 60 Mana

Azir dashes to one of his Sand Soldiers, damaging enemies hit for 60/90/120/150/180 (+40%) magic damage.

If Azir hits an enemy champion, his dash stops and he gains a shield for 4 seconds that absorbs up to 80/120/160/200/240 (+15% of bonus Health) damage.

Emperor's Divide
Range: 250 Cooldown: 140/120/100 Cost: 100 Mana

Azir summons a wall of 4/5/6 armored soldiers that charge forward, knocking back enemies and dealing 150/225/300 (+60%) magic damage. The soldiers then remain as a wall for 3 seconds.

Enemies will be stopped by Emperor's Divide, even if they attempt to dash over the wall, but Azir and his allies can pass freely through it.

Emperor's Divide does not interact with Azir's basic attacks or spells.
