Elise: The Spider Queen Jungle

Spider Queen

Human Form: When Elise's abilities hit an enemy, she gains a dormant Spiderling.

Spider Form: Basic attacks deal bonus magic damage and restore health to Elise.

Neurotoxin / Venomous Bite
Range: 625 / 475 Cooldown: 6 / 6
Cost: 80/85/90/95/100 Mana / No Cost

Human Form: Deals 40/75/110/145/180 (+4% (+[3% Ability Power]%) of target's current health) as magic damage.
Maximum bonus against monsters: 75/100/125/150/175.

Spider Form: Lunges at an enemy and deals 60/100/140/180/220 (+8% (+[3% Ability Power]%) of target's missing health) as magic damage.

Volatile Spiderling / Skittering Frenzy
Range: 950 / 0 Cooldown: 12 / 12
Cost: 60/70/80/90/100 Mana / No Cost

Human Form: Summons a venom-gorged Spiderling that moves to target location and explodes, dealing 60/110/160/210/260 (+80% Ability Power) Magic Damage when it nears an enemy or after 3 seconds.

Spider Form: Elise and her Spiderlings gain Attack Speed.

Cocoon / Rappel
Range: 1075 / 750 Cooldown: 14/13/12/11/10 / 26/23/20/17/14
Cost: 50 Mana / No Cost

Human Form: Stuns the first enemy hit for 1.6/1.7/1.8/1.9/2 seconds and reveals them if they are not stealthed.

Spider Form: Elise and her Spiderlings ascend into the air and then descend upon target enemy.

Spider Form
Cooldown: 4 Cost: No Cost

Human Form: Elise transforms into a menacing spider, sacrificing 425 attack range in exchange for 25 movement speed and access to arachnid abilities. All dormant Spiderlings are awakened and will attack nearby foes.
Spiderlings: Spiderlings deal 10/15/20/25 (+15% Ability Power) damage and take 25% reduced damage from multi-target abilities.

Spider Form: Elise transform into her human form, sacrificing 25 movement speed for 425 attack range and access to her human abilities. In addition, Elise stores all unleashed Spiderlings.
