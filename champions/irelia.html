Irelia: The Will of the Blades Top

Ionian Fervor

Irelia reduces the duration of disables on herself based on whether she is Outnumbered by nearby champions. If she's in an even fight, she'll reduce disables by a small amount, if she's outnumbered, she'll reduce disables by a larger amount.

Bladesurge
Range: 650 Cooldown: 14/12/10/8/6
Cost: 50/55/60/65/70 Mana

Irelia dashes forward to strike her target, dealing 20/50/80/110/140 (+120% Attack Damage) physical damage and applying on-hit effects.If it kills the target, Bladesurge's cooldown is refreshed and Irelia gains 35 Mana.

Hiten Style
Cooldown: 15 Cost: 40 Mana

Passive: Basic attacks restore 5/7/9/11/13 Health.

Active: Irelia's basic attacks deal 15/30/45/60/75 true damage and Hiten Style restores twice as much health for 6 seconds.

Equilibrium Strike
Range: 325 Cooldown: 8
Cost: 50/55/60/65/70 Mana

Irelia pierces her target, dealing 80/120/160/200/240 (+50% Ability Power) magic damage and slowing them by 60% for 1/1.25/1.5/1.75/2 second(s). If the target has a higher Health % than Irelia, she stuns them for the duration instead.

Transcendent Blades
Range: 1000 Cooldown: 110/85/60
Cost: 100 Mana

Irelia summons 4 spirit blades that she can fling to deal 80/120/160 (+70% bonus Attack Damage) (+50% Ability Power) physical damage to enemies they pass through. She heals for 25% of the damage dealt against champions. (10% vs. minions and monsters)
