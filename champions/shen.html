Shen: The Eye of Twilight Top

Ki Barrier

After casting a spell, Shen gets a shield. Affecting other champions reduces the cooldown of this effect.

Twilight Assault
Range: 400 Cooldown: 8/7.25/6.5/5.75/5
Cost: 140/130/120/110/100 Energy

Shen recalls his spirit blade and draws it. Enemies it collides with are slowed by 35% when moving away from Shen for the next 2 seconds. Shen's next 3 attacks deal 2/2.5/3/3.5/4 (+[1.5% Ability Power]%) of their target's max health as bonus magic damage. If the spirit blade collided with an enemy champion, those attacks deal 4/4.5/5/5.5/6 (+[2% Ability Power])% instead and have +50% Attack Speed. Each attack deals 30/50/70/90/110 extra magic damage to monsters and minions (max 75/100/125/150/175).

Spirit's Refuge
Range: 400 Cooldown: 18/16.5/15/13.5/12
Cost: 40 Energy

Shen's spirit blade creates a defensive zone for 1.75 seconds. Basic attacks that would hit Shen or an allied champion in the zone are blocked.If there are no champions to protect in the zone when it starts, the spirit blade will not activate until one enters or 2 seconds pass.

Shadow Dash
Range: 600 Cooldown: 18/16/14/12/10
Cost: 180 Energy

Passive: Dealing damage with Shadow Dash or Twilight Assault recovers 30/35/40 Energy.

Active: Shen dashes in a direction, dealing 50/75/100/125/150 (+12% bonus health) physical damage to enemy champions and neutral monsters in his path and taunting them for 1.5 seconds.

Stand United
Range: Global Cooldown: 180/150/120
Cost: No Cost

Shen grants a shield to a target allied champion that absorbs up to 250/550/850 (+135% Ability Power) damage for 5 seconds. After channeling for 3 seconds, Shen and his spirit blade teleport to the ally's location.
