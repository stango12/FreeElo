Ashe: The Frost Archer Marksman

Frost Shot

Ashe's attacks slow their target, causing her to deal increased damage to these targets. Ashe's critical strikes deal no bonus damage but apply an empowered slow to the target.

Ranger's Focus
Range: 400 Cooldown: 4
Cost: 50 Mana + 4 Focus

Passive: Basic attacks grant Focus for 4 seconds, stacking up to 4 times. Stacks fall off one at a time, and at 4 stacks, Ranger's Focus can be cast, consuming all Focus.
Active: For 4 seconds, Ashe gains 20/25/30/35/40% Attack Speed, and her basic attacks fire a flurry of arrows dealing 21/22/23/24/25% Attack Damage physical damage. During this time, she does not stack Focus. Ranger's Focus applies Frost Shot.

Volley
Range: 1200 Cooldown: 15/12.5/10/7.5/5
Cost: 50 Mana

Ashe fires arrows in a cone, each dealing 20/35/50/65/80 (+100% Attack Damage) physical damage. Enemies can block multiple arrows, but only take damage from the first. Champion hits count as Critical Strikes for the purposes of Frost Shot.

Hawkshot
Range: 25000 Recharge Time: 90/80/70/60/50
Cost: 1 Charge

Ashe sends her Hawk Spirit on a scouting mission anywhere on the map, revealing terrain as it flies toward its target location and granting vision at its target location for 5 seconds. Ashe can store up to 2 charges of Hawkshot.

Enchanted Crystal Arrow
Range: 20000 Cooldown: 100/90/80
Cost: 100 Mana

Ashe launches a crystal arrow of ice that stuns the first enemy Champion hit, dealing 200/400/600 (+100% Ability Power) magic damage. The farther the arrow flies, the longer the stun, up to 3.5 seconds. Surrounding enemies take half the damage.
