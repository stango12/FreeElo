Yorick: Shepherd of Souls Top

Shepherd of Souls

Yorick can raise Mist Walkers with his abilities. Enemies that die near Yorick will sometimes leave a grave.

Last Rites
Cooldown: 7/6.5/6/5.5/5 Cost: 25 Mana

Yorick's next basic attack deals 30/55/80/105/130 (+140% Attack Damage) physical damage and restores 12-82 Health (doubled when below half Health). Last Rites will leave a grave if it kills the target. When there are three or more graves nearby and Last Rites has been used, Yorick can cast Awakening to raise Mist Walkers from graves.

Dark Procession
Range: 600 Cooldown: 20/18/16/14/12
Cost: 70 Mana

Yorick summons a destructible wall of spirits with 2/2/3/3/4 Health around target area for 4 seconds. Allies can walk through the wall freely.

Mourning Mist
Range: 700 Cooldown: 12/11/10/9/8
Cost: 50/55/60/65/70 Mana

Yorick throws a globule of Black Mist that deals 15% of enemy's current Health as magic damage (minimum 70/105/140/175/210 (+70% Ability Power)), slows by 30% for 2 seconds, and marks champions and monsters for 4 seconds. Yorick and his minions are hasted by 20% while moving toward the mark.

Eulogy of the Isles
Range: 600 Cooldown: 160/150/140
Cost: 100 Mana

Yorick summons The Maiden of the Mist and 2/3/4 Mist Walkers. The Maiden has 700/1500/3000 (+15% Maximum Health) Health, deals 10/20/40 (+30% Attack Damage) magic damage, and raises Mist Walkers from nearby enemy deaths. When Yorick damages the Maiden's target he will deal 5/7.5/10% of their max Health as bonus magic damage (2 second cooldown). If the Maiden is summoned inside a lane, she will start pushing the lane.
