Kassadin: The Void Walker Middle

Void Stone

Kassadin takes 15% reduced magic damage and ignores unit collision.

Null Sphere
Range: 650 Cooldown: 9
Cost: 70/75/80/85/90 Mana

Kassadin fires an orb of void energy that deals 65/95/125/155/185 (+70% Ability Power) magic damage and interrupts channels. The excess energy forms around himself, granting a shield that absorbs 40/70/100/130/160 (+30% Ability Power) magic damage for 1.5 seconds.

Nether Blade
Cooldown: 7 Cost: No Mana

Passive: Kassadin's basic attacks draw energy from the void, dealing 20 (+10% Ability Power) bonus magic damage.

Active: Kassadin charges his Nether Blade, causing his next basic attack to deal 40/65/90/115/140 (+70% Ability Power) bonus magic damage and restore 4/5/6/7/8% of his missing Mana (increases to 20/25/30/35/40% against champions).

Force Pulse
Range: 400 Cooldown: 6
Cost: 60/65/70/75/80 Mana

Kassadin draws energy from spells cast in his vicinity, gaining a charge whenever a spell is cast near him. Upon reaching 6 charges, Kassadin can use Force Pulse to deal 80/105/130/155/180 (+70% Ability Power) magic damage and slow enemies by 50/60/70/80/90% for 1 second in a cone in front of him.

Riftwalk
Range: 500 Cooldown: 6/4/2
Cost: 50 Mana

Kassadin teleports to a nearby location dealing 80/100/120 (+2% Maximum Mana) (+20% Ability Power) magic damage to surrounding enemy units. Each subsequent Riftwalk within the next 15 seconds doubles the Mana cost and deals an additional 40/50/60 (+1% Maximum Mana) (+10% Ability Power) magic damage per stack, stacking up to 4 times.
