Twitch: The Plague Rat Marksman

Deadly Venom

Twitch's basic attacks infect the target, dealing true damage each second.

Ambush
Cooldown: 16 Cost: 40 Mana

Twitch becomes Camouflaged and gains 10% Movement Speed for 10/11/12/13/14 seconds. This bonus triples when he is nearby an enemy champion that cannot see him. Twitch gains 30/35/40/45/50% Attack Speed for 5 seconds after exiting Ambush. Ambush resets when a champion dies while affected by Deadly Venom.

Stealth - Camouflage: Twitch is hidden from view while enemy champions remain outside his detection radius. Attacking or casting spells ends Camouflage.

Venom Cask
Range: 950 Cooldown: 13/12/11/10/9
Cost: 70 Mana

Twitch hurls a cask that adds a stack of Deadly Venom to all enemies struck and leaves behind a toxic cloud that persists for 3 seconds. Enemies that remain within the cloud have 25/30/35/40/45% reduced Movement Speed and receive an additional stack of Deadly Venom each second.

Contaminate
Range: 1200 Cooldown: 12/11/10/9/8
Cost: 50/60/70/80/90 Mana

Twitch wreaks further havoc on poisoned enemies with a blast of his vile diseases, dealing 20/35/50/65/80 physical damage plus 15/20/25/30/35 (+20% Ability Power) (+25% bonus Attack Damage) per stack of Deadly Venom to all nearby enemies affected by Deadly Venom.

Spray and Pray
Range: 1200 Cooldown: 90
Cost: 100 Mana

For 5 seconds Twitch gains 300 Attack Range and 20/30/40 Bonus Attack Damage. For the duration, his basic attacks become piercing bolts that deal 20% less damage to subsequent targets, down to a minimum of 40% damage.

Stealth - Camouflage: Activating Spray and Pray does not end Camouflage.
