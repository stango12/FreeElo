Shyvana: The Half-Dragon Jungle

Fury of the Dragonborn

Shyvana deals bonus damage to dragons and gains Armor and Magic Resistance. As Shyvana and her allies slay more dragons, she gains more bonus Armor and Magic Resistance.

Twin Bite
Range: 650 Cooldown: 9/8/7/6/5
Cost: No Cost

Shyvana strikes twice on her next attack, dealing 80/85/90/95/100% Attack Damage physical damage on the first and second hits respectively. While Twin Bite is on cooldown, basic attacks reduce the remaining cooldown duration by 0.5 seconds.

Dragon Form: Twin Bite cleaves all units in front Shyvana. Twin Bite's second hit damage is equal to 40/55/70/85/100% of Shyvana's attack damage.

Burnout
Range: 325 Cooldown: 12
Cost: No Cost

Shyvana deals 20/32/45/57/70 (+20% bonus Attack Damage) (+10% Ability Power) magic damage per second to nearby enemies and gains a bonus 30/35/40/45/50% movement speed that decays over 3 seconds. While Burnout is active, basic attacks deal 5/8/11.25/14.25/17.5 (+5% Bonus Attack Damage) (+2.5% Ability Power) magic damage to nearby enemies and extend its duration by 1 second.

Dragon Form: Burnout grows in size, deals +20% damage to monsters, and has a maximum duration of 7 seconds.

Flame Breath
Range: 925 Cooldown: 12/11/10/9/8
Cost: No Cost

Shyvana unleashes a fireball that stops after hitting a champion. All enemies hit take 60/100/140/180/220 (+30% Ability Power) magic damage and are marked for 5 seconds. Shyvana's basic attacks against marked targets deal 2.5% of their maximum health as magic damage on hit.

Dragon Form: Flame Breath explodes on impact or at target location, dealing 100-200 (+30% Ability Power) bonus magic damage and scorching the earth for 4 seconds. Enemies on scorched earth take 60-120 (+20% Ability Power) magic damage per second. Flame Breath's on-hit damage against monsters is capped at 100 damage per hit.

Dragon's Descent
Range: 850 Cooldown: 12/11/10
Cost: 5 Fury a Second

Passive: Shyvana generates 1/1.5/2 Fury every second. Basic Attacks generate 2 Fury.

Active: Shyvana transforms into a dragon, gaining 150/250/350 Health and flying to target location. Enemies along her path take 150/250/350 (+70% Ability Power) magic damage and are knocked toward her target location.
